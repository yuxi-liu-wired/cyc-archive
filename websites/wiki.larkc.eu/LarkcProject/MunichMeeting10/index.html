1. Travel

Useful facts about Munich are available at Wikitravel. For information about current events, tickets etc. see Munich tourist information.

1.1. To Munich

Excellent information how to get to Munich is contained in the Get in section of Munich's Wikitravel page.

  • by air: Airports Munich Airport [MUC]

  • by rail: The major train stations is München Hauptbahnhof

1.2. Within Munich

  • Public transport site: MVV:

    • The same tickets are valid for the S-Bahn, U-Bahn, trams and buses.
    • There are no barriers, but tickets must be validated by stamping them prior to boarding a train.
    • Ticket recommendation for single people:
      • If you only visit the meeting and arrive at the airport, get single tickets for the trips airport / Starnberg (9.60 € each) (4 Zones) (need to be stamped before entering the S-Bahn)
      • If you only visit the meeting and arrive at the train station get single tickets for the trips Hauptbahnhof / Starnberg (4.80 € each) (2 Zones) (need to be stamped before entering the S-Bahn)

      • If you plan to join the Munich trip, you might consider buying a 3-day ?CityTourCard for singles 29.50 € (?CityTourCard; Single Ticket; 3 days; entire network/Gesamtnetz)

    • Ticket recommendation for people travelling in a group

      • If you are travelling in a group: you can purchase a 3-day ?CityTourCard for partners at 48 € (?CityTourCard; Partner Ticket; 3 days; entire network/Gesamtnetz). This is valid for up to 5 people.

    • If you want to venture into the highly complex Munich ticketing system you are welcome to visit http://www.mvv-muenchen.de/en/home/index.html and tell us about the really best ways to do the trips when you are travelling in groups of different sizes.

2. Venue

The meeting will take place at the Tagungszentrum Starnberger See http://www.dgb-tagungszentren.de/starnberger-see

From the airport: S-Bahn S1 or S8 to Hauptbahnhof (approx. 45 minutes), then S-Bahn S6 to Starnberg Bahnhof (Seebhf.) (approx. 32 minutes)

The meeting side is outside of Munich on the beautiful Starnberger See in Pöcking (Ferdinand-von-Miller-Str. 7, Niederpöcking) , along the S6 line near the S-Bahn station Starnberg Bahnhof (Seebhf.). From there you have three options. 1: Take a taxi (recommended). 2: Take Regionalbus 964 to Niederpöcking (runs infrequently bus 964). 3: Take a 28 minute walk.

Walk from the center to Starnberg, S-Bahnhof (Fussweg.pdf)
1. North on Ferdinand-von-Miller-Straße (<20 min)
2. Keep right on Unterer Seeweg (7Min)
3. This then becomes Seepromenade (9Min)

Taxi-in Starnberg:

  • Taxi am Bahnhof 08151 - 12313

  • Taxi Moni 08151 - 744 777
